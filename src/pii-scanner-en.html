What Is Personal Identifiable Information?

Any data that can be used to identify an individual...

This includes names, dates of birth, addresses, contact details, identification numbers, or financial information. This data is commonly used by banks and other institutions to identify customers, but is often inadvertently exposed in a user's social media posts.

Financial fraudsters can leverage this data to bypass security checks and gain access to mobile banking services, giving scammers unfettered access to a user's account.

To avoid becoming the victim of such fraud, users should take particular care when making posts online. Social Spiders can help to indicate where Personally Identifiable Information has previously been exposed on a user's social media.
